## Project Description

Our platform allows students to **ask doubts anonymously**, get **peer replies**, and **upvote helpful answers**, fostering a collaborative and fear-free learning environment.

---

## The Problem Statement

Many students hesitate to ask doubts in class due to **fear of embarrassment** or **peer pressure**, resulting in gaps in understanding.

---

## The Solution

We provide a **safe and anonymous platform** where students can post their doubts, receive answers from peers or mentors, and vote for the most helpful responses, ensuring better engagement and learning.

## Features

* **Anonymous Doubt Posting:** Ask questions without revealing your identity.
* **Peer Replies:** Get answers from classmates or mentors.
* **Upvoting System:** Upvote the most helpful answers.
* **Search & Categories:** Filter doubts by subject or keywords.
* **Real-time Updates:** New doubts and answers appear instantly.

**System Architecture:**

* Users (Students) interact with the web interface.
* Backend (Node.js + Firebase) handles authentication, doubt storage, and real-time updates.
* Frontend displays doubts, answers, and votes dynamically.

**Application Workflow:**

1. Student logs in anonymously.
2. Posts a doubt or browses existing doubts.
3. Peers respond or upvote answers.
4. Real-time updates are reflected on the dashboard.
